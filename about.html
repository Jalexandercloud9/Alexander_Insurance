Where I Come From

I grew up in inner city New Orleans and experienced financial hardship early on. For a long time, I was doing what most people do — working hard without a clear financial path.

That changed when I was equipped with the right knowledge.

Once I understood how money actually works, everything shifted. I was able to create stability for myself and build a foundation for my wife and our six children.

Why I Do This

That is why I do this. I believe this is knowledge everyone should have, not just a select few.

My focus is simple: understand where you are today, identify what is missing, and give you the tools to move forward with clarity.

"This is not about selling a policy. It is about building something that works for your life."
